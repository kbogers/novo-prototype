Discover clinical trials for rare blood disorders

At Novo Nordisk, our scientists are currently working on novel and innovative treatments to address the unmet needs of people living with rare blood disorders, and other serious chronic diseases.

How it works

Taking part in a research study can feel overwhelming. We’ve simplified the first steps to make it as easy as possible to learn more.

  1. 1

    Share your details

    Start by answering a few questions in our short, confidential online questionnaire. This helps our team understand if a clinical trial might be a good fit for you.

  2. 2

    Check your eligibility

    Our online pre-screener gives you an immediate preliminary check. This saves you time and helps us connect you with the right people.

  3. 3

    Talk with a patient navigator

    If you appear to be a potential match, you can then book a free, no-obligation phone call with a member of our patient navigator team. This call is your opportunity to ask questions and get more details about our trials, with absolutely no commitment to join. Support is available in your language, and interpreters can be provided if needed. Your information is kept private every step of the way.
